## Insertion Blueprint

### 段落承诺（Chapter Promise）
- 证明：丰裕的悖论有镜像面——系统不只销毁它本可分配的盈余，还主动制造身体吸收不了的盈余，再把"管理后果"做成第二门生意。
- 读者为何在意：把全书"稀缺是制度产物"的命题推到极致——盈余同样是被设计的；人体是两套产业之间的缓冲器。
- 战略角色：为 Agency 章（代谢能动性可被化学外包）和序言修订（净收益 + 过渡成本须治理）提供最锋利、可指认的具象案例。

### Section Logic
1. 开题张力：丰裕不只销毁盈余，还制造吸收不了的盈余并售卖其管理
2. 核心论证：食物系统工程化过度消费（UPF > 一半热量）→ 身体承压 → 制药业接手
3. 支撑证据：肥胖 10 亿（Lancet 2024）、UPF > 一半热量（CDC 2025）、美国 1/8 成人用药（KFF 2025）、市场 600–660 亿→1850–3000 亿（Grand View / SNS 2025–26）、年费 ~1.1–1.2 万刀（NCSL 2026）
4. 机制修正 + 视角转换：药物非"燃脂炉"，而是把最底层代谢能动性外包给一管肽；停药反弹 = 终身代谢义肢
5. 收束：药非恶，系统失败在"两端都变现"；稀缺与盈余都是被制造的

## Versioned Chapter Draft

### Ch6 §IV Extension — Version 1 — ready for review (EN)

> The abundance we have built does not only discard what it could distribute. It also manufactures what the body cannot absorb — and then sells the management of the consequence.
>
> In 2022, more than one billion people on this planet were living with obesity (Lancet / NCD-Risk Collaboration, 2024). In the United States, more than half of all calories now come from ultra-processed foods (CDC, NCHS Data Brief 536, 2025). These two facts are not separate. The same food system that lets a third of the global harvest rot in warehouses has engineered the other end of the pipeline into a product calibrated to a "bliss point" of salt, sugar, and fat the body is not built to refuse.
>
> And when the body breaks under the surplus, a second industry arrives to manage the damage. GLP-1 receptor agonists — semaglutide, tirzepatide — are now prescribed to roughly one in eight American adults, and prescriptions have tripled since 2020 (KFF, RAND, 2025). A market valued at sixty to sixty-six billion dollars in 2025 is projected to reach between 185 and 300 billion by the mid-2030s (Grand View, SNS Insider). At list price, a year of treatment runs roughly eleven to twelve thousand dollars per patient (NCSL, 2026).
>
> Here the mechanism must be stated precisely, because imprecision is exactly what the critics of this book wait for. These drugs do not turn the body into a calorie furnace. They act on the hypothalamus to suppress appetite, slow gastric emptying to prolong satiety, and modulate the brain's reward circuitry. Weight loss comes overwhelmingly from eating less, not from burning more. The accurate claim is narrower and more unsettling than the myth: the system has outsourced a person's most basic metabolic agency — the age-old human capacity to feel hunger, to stop, to choose — to a weekly injection.
>
> And the outsourcing is not temporary. Discontinuation of semaglutide is associated with pronounced rebound in body weight and blood pressure (Lancet EClinicalMedicine, 2025). The body, having surrendered its metabolic self-regulation to the peptide, takes the weight back when the peptide leaves. This is not a cure. It is a lifelong metabolic prosthesis — planned obsolescence of the body itself, where the device that must be maintained forever is the person.
>
> None of this makes the medicine villainous. In the SELECT trial, semaglutide cut major cardiovascular events by twenty percent (NEJM, 2023). For the diabetic, the hypertensive, the genuinely metabolically trapped, the drug is a real emancipation. The failure is systemic, not pharmaceutical: a civilization that engineers food for overconsumption and then engineers a molecule to manage the fallout — but will not engineer the food environment itself — has chosen to monetize both ends of the loop. The body becomes the buffer between two profit centers.
>
> This is the mirror image of the abundance thesis. Scarcity was never natural; it was manufactured. So, it turns out, is surplus. And the same system that profits from each has no interest in letting either disappear.

### Ch6 §IV 延伸 — Version 1 — 待审（中文）

> 我们所建的丰裕，不只销毁它本可分配的东西。它还会制造身体根本吸收不了的东西——然后再把"管理后果"做成一门生意。
>
> 2022 年，这颗星球上超过 10 亿人正被肥胖困扰（Lancet / NCD-Risk Collaboration，2024）。在美国，国民热量摄入已超过一半来自超加工食品（CDC，NCHS Data Brief 536，2025）。这两件事不是分开的。同一个让全球三分之一收成在仓库里腐烂的食物系统，把管道的另一端也工程化了——按盐、糖、脂肪的"极乐点"调校，做成人体本能无法拒绝的产品。
>
> 而当身体被这股盈余压垮，第二门产业就来"管理损伤"。GLP-1 受体激动剂——司美格鲁肽、替尔泊肽——已用于约八分之一的美国成年人，处方量自 2020 年翻了三倍（KFF、RAND，2025）。这个 2025 年估值 600–660 亿美元的市场，预计到 2030 年代中期将达到 1850–3000 亿美元（Grand View、SNS Insider）。按标价，一年疗程每人约需 1.1–1.2 万美元（NCSL，2026）。
>
> 这里的机制必须说准，因为模糊恰好是本书批评者等待的破绽。这类药物并没有把人体变成热量焚化炉。它们作用于下丘脑抑制食欲、延缓胃排空以延长饱腹、并调控大脑的奖赏回路。减重主要来自"吃得少"，而非"烧得多"。准确的断言比神话更窄，也更令人不安：系统把一个人最底层的代谢能动性——感知饥饿、知道停手、做出选择这一人类沿用万年的能力——外包给了一管每周注射的肽。
>
> 而且这外包不是暂时的。停用司美格鲁肽后，体重与血压会出现显著反弹（Lancet EClinicalMedicine，2025）。身体把代谢自我调节交出去之后，肽一离开，体重就回来了。这不是治愈。这是一具终身的代谢义肢——身体的"计划性报废"，而那台必须永远维护的设备，就是人自己。
>
> 这不代表药是恶的。SELECT 试验中，司美格鲁肽把主要心血管事件降低了 20%（NEJM，2023）。对糖尿病患者、高血压者、真正被代谢困住的人，它是真实的 liberation。失败在系统，不在药：一个能把食物工程化为过度消费、再工程化出一分子管理其后果，却不愿去工程化食物环境本身的文明，是主动选择把闭环的两端都变现。身体成了两个利润中心之间的缓冲器。
>
> 这是丰裕命题的镜像。稀缺从来不是自然的，它是被制造的。如今看来，盈余也是。而同一个从两端获利的系统，对让任何一端消失都没有兴趣。
